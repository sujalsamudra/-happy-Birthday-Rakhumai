Ab main utna udaas nahi hoon, aur na hi poori tarah khush… bas kahin beech mein hoon, seekh raha hoon, aage badh raha hoon. Zindagi ab theek hai, dheere-dheere sab samajh aane laga hai… maine akele rehna seekh liya hai, khamosh dinon mein bhi mazboot rehna seekh liya hai… par kuch yaadein kabhi khatam nahi hoti, bas khamosh ho jaati hain. Dil toota hai, lekin uske har tukde mein aaj bhi tera naam goonjta hai… Kabhi khwaab tha ki har pal tere saath bitaun, apni har bewajah si baatein tujhe bataun… aur tu bas muskura kar kahe, “sab theek ho jayega.” Kabhi-kabhi sochta hoon, agar tum aaj bhi saath hote to sab kitna alag hota… phir khud ko samjha leta hoon ki har cheez hamesha ke liye nahi hoti. Guzre din, guzri raat, par teri yaad kam na hui… bohot roka, phir bhi teri yaad aana band na hui… kis kadar ki hai aashiqi hamari… aisi koi raat nahi, jis raat aankhen hamari num na hui… Main theek ho raha hoon… sach mein… par is sab ke beech, kabhi-kabhi aaj bhi teri yaad aa hi jaati hai.
